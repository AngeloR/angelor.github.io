Rising Legends [Garden]

[ project ] [ rising-legends ] [ garden ]

This page will serve as the current state of Rising Legends, a Persistent Browser-Based Game that I’m working on.

Skills

All users get the “core” skills: bladed, blunt, one handed mastery, two handed mastery, destruction magic, restoration magic, light armour mastery, heavy armour mastery. These are all passive and improve based on use.

Professions

Wanderer

The primary profession. All players start out as a wanderer. Wanderers gain stat point increases every other level.Â 

Warrior

+2 STR on even levels, +1 STR, +1 CON on odd

Rogue

+2 DEX on even levels, +1 DEX, +1 INT on odd

Mage

+2 INT on even levels, +1 INT, +1 CON on odd

News

2023-05-07

Started the rework on inventory to support updating skills dynamically. All skills are “affected by” a skill. For example, a Short Sword is affected by your One Handed Mastery skill and your Bladed Mastery skill.
