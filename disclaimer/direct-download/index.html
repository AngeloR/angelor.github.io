Verify Copyright Despite Direct Download Link

We have given a direct download link to the book/work, since this is, to our understanding, permitted in this case. Regardless we strongly recommend that you do not just download from the provided link, but check the copyright status (what you are allowed to do with the book/work) on the homepage of the book/work.
