My i3wm Setup

I’ve recently made the switch from the stock Ubuntu setup over to i3wm. I’ve used xmonad for many years before this, but always found it a bit harder than I would have liked to customize it.

Most of what i3 does out of the box works perfectly for me. The only thing I didn’t like was the fact that navigation was shifted from hjkl to jkl;. I’m also not a big fan of dmenu. Honestly I’m not totally sure why, but I’ve always struggled getting it to do what I wanted.

With i3 I made a few small config changes, and swapped out the launcher and so far things have been quite excellent. I’m just posting this here so that I don’t forget my configs.
